Common Issues Timeline

Frequented alert symptoms reported by Rocsta owners.

Excessive oil consumption and blue smoke

Oil consumption exceeding 1.5 liters per 1000 km. Blue smoke from the exhaust when accelerating after prolonged idling or during cold starts.

!

Transfer case lever stuck in 4WD

Unable to move the small transfer case lever back to 2H after driving on trails in 4H or 4L. Feels stiff or mechanically stuck.

i

Speedometer fluctuates or does not work

The speedometer needle fluctuates wildly at low speed or stays at zero permanently. The odometer also stops advancing.
